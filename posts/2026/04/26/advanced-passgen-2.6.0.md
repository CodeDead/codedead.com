## Information

![Advanced PassGen](https://i.imgur.com/WcaJL2t.png)

Advanced PassGen v2.6.0 has just been released. We have been working hard to improve the performance and stability of the application, and we are excited to share the latest version with you.
This release includes mostly bug fixes and minor improvements. For instance, we've updated to Tauri v2 and issue with nvidia gpu's has been fixed on Linux.

For a full list of changes, please see the changelog below.
