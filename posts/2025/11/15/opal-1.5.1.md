## Information

![Opal](https://i.imgur.com/VbN1wnN.png)

Opal v1.5.1 has just been released. This release includes performance and security improvements to enhance your experience. The community has also improved some of the translations.
In addition, a new soundtrack has been added to provide more variety for your projects!

- translate fixes and refindments by @LightBlueCube in [https://github.com/CodeDead/opal/pull/144](https://github.com/CodeDead/opal/pull/144)
